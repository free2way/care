# TechGuard Senior Care Console

TechGuard is a multi-agent care application for older adults who live independently and for the family members who support them. The app separates the senior view from the family view, so seniors see simple daily reminders while caregivers can configure medication schedules, tasks, travel plans, weather care, health records, and Feishu group notifications.

## What It Does

- **Medication Agent**: lets caregivers set what medicine should be taken and at what time. Seniors can confirm when they have taken it.
- **Task Agent**: lets family members assign daily tasks, such as measuring blood pressure or preparing for an outing.
- **Reminder Agent**: scans scheduled tasks and medication records, then sends Feishu group alerts when important reminders are due.
- **Fraud Protection Agent**: checks suspicious SMS text pasted by the senior and explains the risk in simple language.
- **Weather and Travel Agent**: creates easy-to-understand travel advice and weather care messages.
- **Health Agent**: records blood pressure, shows trends, and supports checkup report photo review.
- **Notification Agent**: sends important events to the family notification center and the Feishu care group.

## Multi-Agent Highlight

TechGuard is designed as a practical multi-agent workflow. Different agents handle different responsibilities: understanding intent, checking safety risks, managing reminders, reading health data, planning travel, and notifying family members. These agents work together like a small care team: one agent collects the context, another agent makes a judgment, another agent stores the record, and another agent sends the right message to the right person.

This makes the app more useful than a single chatbot. It can turn family instructions into scheduled actions, turn senior input into understandable guidance, and turn urgent events into real-time family alerts.

## Social Value

Many older adults face two problems at the same time: digital services are hard to use, and online fraud is becoming more complicated. TechGuard helps reduce that pressure. It gives seniors a calmer, simpler interface, and it helps family members provide care without needing to constantly check in manually.

The goal is not to replace family care. The goal is to make care more timely, organized, and warm. With this kind of tool, technology can help older adults live more safely and confidently, while giving families more peace of mind.
